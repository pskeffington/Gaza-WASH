## Manuscript admission rule

A manuscript sentence may move from scaffold language to finding language only when the claim has: source ID, claim ID, allowed status, exact page or table location, and scope wording that matches the source role.

## Blocked language

Avoid Gaza-wide prevalence language from local factsheets. Avoid household outcome language from infrastructure recovery reports. Avoid current field-condition language from background mechanism literature.
